Research

The following is the reasearch carried out in order to build our website.

The Camera Store

We were inspired by thecamerastore.com to highlight our ongoing sales in such a way, but ultimately decided that the designs appears congested so we decided to add a horizontal scroll bar to make it a visually appleaing, responsive and interactive design.

Hukut

We decided take inspiration from hukut.com to add a zebra table to describe specifications of the products to create website that is able to compete in the marketplace.

The Pilgrims Books

We were inspired by the e-commerce website of The Pilgrims Books to add responsive and automatic image sliders in both our home page and blog page as it not only visually appealing but it also gives many promotional oppourtunites. For our image sliders we decided to simplify them by making them fully automatic instead of interactive.

Daraz

We wanted to replicate the slideshow gallery as designed in the Daraz website but decided but decided to simplify it by creating a simple interactive slideshow and adding pictures under it to give the appearance of slideshow gallery.

The Camera Store

The save label in our product page was directly inspired from the product cards of The camera store. We decided to replicate it directly but positioned our label on the top as our product card is much smaller in height.
