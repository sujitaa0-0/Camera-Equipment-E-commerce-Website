About Us

"Camera Corner" Company is the result of a technological revolution and growing e-commerce industry, that encouraged us to create “A One-Stop Digital Platform”, for all camera lovers. We sell cameras ensuring quality and with the promise of its performance. But above all, one thing we prioritize in selling is “ The best camera and filming accessories for you”.

Our Story

A story of many firsts and every one of them was a blessing. camera corner began operation in Kathmandu in 2021 December as the first complete online one-click online platform in Nepal with a store in Balaju. The same year, we became the first camera and accessories retail store in the industry to introduce the concept of showrooms. Our company has provided best out of best and has convinced many customers and cinematography enthusiast , hence we provide best out of best camera equipment that you will get in the market or you can visit our store for wide range of varieties.
